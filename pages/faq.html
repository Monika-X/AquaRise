Quick Answers

Popular Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions

What is the minimum age to join AquaRise?

Children as young as 3 years old can join our parent-toddler water introduction program. For independent swimming classes, the minimum age is 4 years. We also have dedicated programs for teens and adults with no upper age limit.

Do beginners need prior swimming experience?

Not at all. Our beginner programs are designed for absolute non-swimmers. Our certified coaches use a progressive approach starting with water confidence, breath control, and basic floating before moving to stroke techniques.

Are trial classes available?

Yes, we offer a complimentary trial assessment for every new student. This helps our coaches evaluate your current swimming level and recommend the most suitable program. Contact our admissions team to schedule your free trial.

How long is each swimming session?

Standard sessions run for 45 minutes for beginners and kids, 60 minutes for intermediate and advanced levels, and 90 minutes for competitive squad training. Private coaching sessions can be customized to your needs.

Is swimming equipment provided?

Yes, we provide all training equipment including kickboards, pull buoys, fins, paddles, and snorkels. Students just need to bring their own swimwear, goggles, and a towel. Swim caps are available at our pro shop.

Do you have female coaches?

Absolutely. We have a diverse team of certified coaches including female instructors. We also offer women-only batch sessions for those who prefer a female-only learning environment.

Are private coaching sessions available?

Yes, we offer one-on-one private coaching sessions tailored to your specific goals. These sessions are ideal for accelerated learning, stroke correction, or competition preparation. Contact us to schedule with your preferred coach.

How do I register for a program?

You can register by filling out the enrollment enquiry form on our Contact page, calling us directly, or visiting the academy in person. Our admissions team will guide you through program selection and registration process.

Facilities & Safety

Facilities & Safety FAQ

Learn about our world-class facilities and stringent safety protocols

What safety measures are in place at the pool?

Our facility is equipped with certified lifeguards on duty at all times, modern pool covers, anti-slip surfaces, clearly marked depth zones, emergency alarms, and first-aid stations. Regular safety drills are conducted with staff and students.

Are locker rooms and changing facilities available?

Yes, we have spacious, clean locker rooms with secure electronic lockers, hot showers, changing areas, and hair dryers. Separate facilities are available for men, women, and families.

Is parking available at the academy?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site parking for all students and visitors with over 100 parking spots. We also have dedicated bicycle parking and easy access to public transportation.

How is the water quality maintained?

Our pools use advanced filtration and UV sanitation systems. Water quality is tested every 2 hours for pH, chlorine, and temperature levels. All results are displayed on a digital board in the facility.

Is first aid available on site?

Yes, we have a fully equipped first-aid room with trained medical staff on site during operating hours. All coaches are certified in CPR, first aid, and emergency water rescue.

Are lifeguards always present during sessions?

Absolutely. Certified lifeguards are stationed at the poolside during all operating hours. Our student-to-lifeguard ratio always exceeds safety regulations for maximum vigilance.
