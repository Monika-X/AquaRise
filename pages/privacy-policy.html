Privacy Policy

AquaRise Aquatic Center is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

01

Introduction

At AquaRise Aquatic Center, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our academy services.

We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is collected and processed in a transparent, lawful, and secure manner. By using our website and services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please do not hesitate to contact our team.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our swimming programs and services. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Age
  • Program Selection

Automatically Collected

  • Browser Type & Version
  • Device Information
  • IP Address
  • Usage & Browsing Data
  • Cookies & Tracking

We only collect information that is necessary for providing our services and improving your experience.

03

How We Use Information

Your information helps us deliver a personalized and seamless experience. We use collected data for the following purposes:

Process Enquiries

Respond to your questions and requests promptly.

Manage Enrollments

Handle registrations and program admissions.

Provide Programs

Deliver swimming coaching and training services.

Send Updates

Share program news, events, and offers.

Improve Experience

Enhance website usability and content relevance.

Customer Support

Provide assistance and resolve issues.

04

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Secure Storage

Data stored on encrypted servers with restricted access.

Encryption

All data transmitted via SSL/TLS encryption protocols.

Restricted Access

Only authorized personnel can access personal data.

Regular Security Checks

Periodic audits and vulnerability assessments.

Safe Payments

Payment data handled through PCI-compliant gateways.

Privacy Protection

Your data is never sold or shared without consent.

Our Commitment to Security

AquaRise maintains a comprehensive data protection program aligned with industry best practices. We conduct regular staff training on data privacy and security protocols. If we become aware of any data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by applicable regulations.

05

Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting our support team.

Access Data

Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.

Request Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Request Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

Opt-out of Comms

Unsubscribe from marketing communications.

Contact Support

Reach out for any privacy-related inquiries.

06

Contact & Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
