Pro Advice

Expert Tips from Our Coaches

Quick actionable advice used by AquaRise coaches to help swimmers at every level improve faster.

Warm-up Routine

Dynamic stretches and light swimming to activate muscles before intense sets.

Stroke Improvement

Focus on high-elbow catch and early vertical forearm for better propulsion.

Breathing Technique

Practice bilateral breathing to balance your stroke and improve oxygen flow.

Recovery Tips

Active recovery swims, stretching, and adequate sleep for muscle repair.

Hydration

Drink water before, during, and after practice even in the pool environment.

Nutrition

Balanced meals with protein and complex carbs within 30 minutes post-swim.
