Pool Rules & Safety

All students and visitors must adhere to the following pool rules and safety regulations to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone:

Pool Dress Code

Proper swimwear is mandatory. Street clothes, denim, and non-swim fabrics are not permitted.

Pool Hygiene

All swimmers must shower before entering the pool. Persons with contagious conditions are not permitted.

Coach Instructions

Students must follow all coach instructions for safety and effective training.

Photography Policy

Photography and videography require prior permission. Respect others' privacy at all times.

Lifeguard Rules

Lifeguard instructions must be obeyed immediately. No diving in shallow areas.

Health Requirements

Students with medical conditions must inform the academy. A medical certificate may be required.

Emergency Procedures

Follow emergency evacuation routes and assembly points as directed by staff.
