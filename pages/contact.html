FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining AquaRise

Do you provide beginner classes?

Yes, we offer beginner classes for all age groups. Our certified coaches use a step-by-step approach to build water confidence and fundamental skills in a safe environment.

What is the minimum age?

Children as young as 3 years old can join our parent-toddler water introduction program. For independent classes, the minimum age is 4 years.

Do you offer trial classes?

Absolutely! We provide a complimentary trial assessment for every new student. This helps us evaluate your current level and recommend the right program.

How can I enroll?

Simply fill out the enquiry form above, call us, or visit the academy in person. Our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process.
